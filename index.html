How to Choose the Right Sportswear

Men's sportswear

The first thing you should know is what discipline you are going to practice since the clothing must match it. The most important thing is that you feel comfortable and enjoy physical exercise. Therefore, dress according to the sport you practice. For example, if you practice spinning or cycling, it is understandable that you wear tighter clothing than in aerobic sports. Polyester, spandex, nylon; Bamboo and wool are also the most recommended fabrics. The clothing must always be according to the period of the year in which you do the sport. It will not be difficult for you to find affordable and good quality sports clothing, sports brands are continually innovating and bringing new models to the market.

Women's sportswear

Sports pants include joggers, synthetic shorts, classic cotton sports pants and leggings. The pants you choose will depend on the discipline you are going to practice. The jogger is ideal for low-intensity exercises, such as Pilates and yoga. It is advisable to wear shorts on summer days during outdoor sports and, without a doubt, they are the most suitable for running. Sports pants are conducive to smooth and constant movements. With tights you have to know how to choose. Classic tights are recommended for any activity as they facilitate mobility and are very comfortable. Capris are perfect for cycling and running. There are also short tights, recommended for use on the hottest days and high-intensity sports. The garment that worries women the most is the bra. Many use the wrong size, or an inappropriate model for their breast type. Women have many doubts when buying this garment. We explain how to choose the bra for sports. The most important thing for any type of bra is that it fits your size. To check that this is the case, we recommend that you try it on before buying it and check that you move without impediment, the fabric does not twist, you breathe well and your breasts remain fixed..
